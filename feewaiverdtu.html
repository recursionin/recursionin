Fee Waiver

As a part of our scholarship program, we are providing fee waivers to top performers from every college.


Scholarship Criteria

Your test score will not only help you in getting admission in our course but will also set the criteria for the equivalent fee waiver.

  • Top 5% performers get 50% waiver
  • Top 15% performers get 25% waiver
  • Top 25% performers get 10% waiver

The total amount that you have to pay after getting a job will be reduced by the percentage equal to the scholarship you win in the test.
