FAQ

If you have any questions about the curriculum, the payment model or anything related to your career as a software developer, we are here to help you.

Course Curriculum and Timings

Who is eligible for this course?

This course focusses only on technical jobs. Only 3rd-year B.Tech. students from Tier 1 and Tier 2 colleges, who are interested in technical jobs are eligible for this course.

What is the duration of this course?

This course will cover all domains of computer science that will be needed to excel in a technical interview. We will cover all the topics in 6 to 7 months. If any student needs further guidance, we will provide mentorship and career counseling after the course also.

What are the timings of this course?

On weekdays, classes will be after college hours, i.e., 6:15 pm to 8:15 pm. On weekends, classes will be from 11:00 am to 4:00 pm. However, mentors and instructors will be available for the help of the students after classes also.

Where will the classes be conducted?

We will conduct the classes on the college campus so that students don't have to waste any time commuting to another location.

Can the course be done along with college?

Yes, we have arranged the class timings such that they don't hinder with your college schedule and don't affect your performance in the college. We will also provide preparatory holidays for your semester examinations. Our curriculum will also help you in performing better in computer-related subjects in your college.

At the end of this course, will you provide any course completion certificate?

Yes, every student who completes this course will receive a certificate from Recursion.

I am experienced. Can I still take this course?

Our course is designed to help every student in improving technical skills. We will start from the basics and move to advanced topics so that you can see significant improvement in your skillset after completing this course, no matter where you stand today.

How do I get my doubts resolved?

Our instructors and mentors will be available after classes to resolve your doubts. You can also contact your mentors online if you need any help.

When does the course start?

We will start our 2019 batch classes from 1st October.

Entrance Test

Why is there an entrance test?

We are conducting a test to ensure that you can efficiently consume the content that we have designed for you. We will also analyze the results to figure out your weak areas to improve your performance. We will recommend you what to study so that you can perform better.

What will be asked in the entrance test?

We are conducting a test to check your overall programming skills. The test will consist of basic coding questions, computer science fundamentals, puzzles, etc. After the test, we will invite you for an interview to further assess your technical knowledge.

Payment Plans

What are the payment options?

We offer two payment plans - prepaid and postpaid. You can either pay for the course with a down payment of ₹50 thousand and pay only 7% salary after you get a job (until you reach a cap of ₹50 thousand). If you decide to choose the postpaid model, you can pay us 14% of your salary when you get a job of more than ₹7 lakh CTC (for 1.5 years or until you reach a ₹1.5 Lakh cap).

What is the meaning of maximum cap of ₹1.5 Lakh or ₹50 thousand?

Maximum cap is the maximum total amount of fee collected, irrespective of your CTC. If you get a job that pays ₹25L,14% of that becomes ₹3.5L. We will not collect ₹3.5L, but stop collecting as soon as the total amount becomes ₹1.5L (in the postpaid plan) or ₹50 thousand (in the prepaid plan).

What will the percentage of 14% be of, base salary or CTC?

You'll be charged 14% of your base salary. You'll have to pay the amount only if you get a job of CTC more than ₹7 Lakh.

What if I opt for the postpaid plan and don't get a job offer?

It will be highly unlikely that you won't get a job after taking this course. In case you can't get a job through our referrals and campus placements and remain unemployed, you don't need to pay us anything.

Does my fee increase as my salary grows?

No, your appraisals or salary hikes based on your job performance or switching the company later don't affect the fee that you pay us monthly.

What if I get placed in a company myself or through any other source apart from Recursion?

Job is the end-result we all are working on together. We all will be working hard to upskill you and prepare you for your selection process. Even if you get a job through other channels, it will be considered a job only, and all the conditions will be unchanged.

What CTC can I expect to get after taking this course?

We will provide you a minimum salary guarantee based on your profile and performance in the course. If we can't get you a job higher than promised minimum guarantee, you will not be liable to pay us anything.
