Our Program

Coding teams, daily stand-ups, experienced instructors, and mentors will enhance your experience at Recursion as you learn through our classroom program.


t

Coding Team & Daily Stand-Ups

Learning to code on your own can be lonely and difficult. At Recursion, you’ll collaborate with other brilliant students and learn along with them.

Every day, you and your team will support each other for higher productivity. Daily stand-ups will ensure that no one gets left behind.

Experienced Instructors & Dedicated Mentors

In addition to your coding and stand-up team, you’ll also get different mentors to help you in coding, projects, resume building, and enhancing your interviewing skills.

From reviewing your code and answering tough questions to helping you get the best job offers, we will be there for you throughout the program.

Weekly Schedule

At Recursion, we ensure that you can learn without hindering your performance in college and semester examinations.

On weekdays, you’ll practice challenging problems with your team. On weekends, you will learn new topics that you will practice the coming week. You will always have the support of your team, instructors, and mentors in developing valuable skills that employers look for in candidates.
